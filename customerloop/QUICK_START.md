## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Business Owners

### Best Practices

1. **Add Customers Immediately**
   - Register new customers on their first visit
   - Collect phone number at minimum
   - Email is optional but helpful

2. **Record Every Visit**
   - One tap to record visit
   - Don't forget to award points
   - Builds accurate customer history

3. **Monitor Your Stats**
   - Check dashboard daily
   - Track repeat customer percentage
   - Aim for higher avg visits per customer

4. **Reward Loyal Customers**
   - Notice customers with high points
   - Offer special deals to repeat visitors
   - Thank customers with "Loyal" badge

5. **Keep Data Updated**
   - Pull to refresh for latest stats
   - Update customer info if changed
   - Maintain accurate records

### Success Metrics to Watch

| Metric | Good Target | Excellent |
|--------|------------|-----------|
| Repeat Customer % | > 30% | > 50% |
| Avg Visits/Customer | > 2.0 | > 3.0 |
| Total Customers | Growing | 100+ |
| Customers with 5+ visits | 10+ | 25+ |

## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### "No customers yet" Empty State
âœ… **This is normal for new businesses**
- Click "Add First Customer"
- Start building your database
- Growth takes time

### Customer Not Appearing
1. Pull down to refresh
2. Check internet connection
3. Verify customer was added successfully
4. Check if you're logged in correctly

### Stats Not Updating
1. Pull down to refresh dashboard
2. Wait 2-3 seconds for sync
3. Check Firebase connection
4. Try logging out and back in

### Can't Record Visit
1. Ensure customer exists
2. Check internet connection
3. Verify you're logged in
4. Try closing and reopening customer dialog
